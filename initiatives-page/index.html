2016
village Gadhaiya, UP
Gadhaiya is a small village in the Rampur district of western Uttar Pradesh with a population of approximately 2000 people. The most common occupations are farming and labour. The camp was organised in collaboration with an NGO, Jaago India which is a people’s movement for building leaders among the society. Jaago India has been consistently working for the development of Gadhaiya
2017
village Harchandpur Kalan
We focussed on certain issues of prime importance such as Open defecation, Hygiene, Education, Career, govt. scheme implementation, etc. by organising various activities in the village. Harchandpur Kalan is a small village in the Etah district of western Uttar Pradesh with a population of approximately 1500 people
2018
villages of Bheekampura and Gopalpura, Rajasthan
Though the goal of this camp was to broaden our understanding of the conditions prevailing in these villages by living with them for some time, we were also successful in making some contributions to the village by means of solar panel installation and informative artwork in the community hall
2019
Gopalpura, Alwar
Among other activities like learning about organic farming practices, setting up library facilities at the community school, offering 'Shramdaan,’ and many more, the volunteers interacted and learned from the 'Waterman of India', Sh. Rajendra Singh regarding various water preservation and river revival techniques.
